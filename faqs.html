FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)
DHAN DHOOM
WHAT IS DHAN DHOOM?

Dhan Dhoom, or DD for short, is a next level fantasy game where fantasy meets pack openings. Instead of having a set budget to choose your squad, you must build your team and set your lineups using cards you have received from packs.

Contests operate on a game week basis, so you can leave those bad game weeks in the past and start afresh with the next game week.

ARE THERE ANY AGE RESTRICTIONS ON DHAN DHOOM?

Yes! You must be 18+ in order to play. We conduct age verification checks as well so don’t try sneaking in if you’re under 18.

HOW MUCH DOES IT COST TO PLAY?

Dhan Dhoom is free to download from the Apple App Store and Google Play. All fantasy game contests in-app are free-to-enter, whilst there’s a small in-app currency fee (DD Coins) to take part in the premium contests.

WHAT DO I RECEIVE UPON SIGN-UP?

Once you have downloaded the application, registered (either as a new user) and entered the game, you will receive a free pack of random player/team cards to enable you to build your first Dhan Dhoom squad/team.

Your initial free pack will include Gold cards, Silver cards and Base cards. The total number of cards and the split between the Gold, Siver & Base cards will be subject to the format of the game i.e., for cricket, T10, T20, One Day International, Test, County, IPL etc; subject to the game, league, contest; season, matches and subject to the discretion of Dhan Dhoom / MysticPlay Studios management. Please refer to the application to find out the composition of your free pack.

Every 6 hours, a user will receive a free pack. If this pack is not claimed it will be replaced by a new free pack. This offer is subject to change and at the discretion of Dhan Dhoom and/or MysticPlay Studios Management.

You will also receive free DD Gold coins within the free pack, every 6 hours to get you started in the marketplace. Again, if not claimed the pack is replaced by a new free pack including DD Gold coins. To be very clear, there is no accumulation if not claimed. Spend them on packs or cards to build your squad / team. This offer is subject to change and at the discretion of Dhan Dhoom and/or MysticPlay Studios Management.

HOW DO I BUILD MY DD SQUAD AND ENTER THE CONTESTS?

It’s super simple, see our step-by-step guide https://dhandhoom.com/gameplay.

All fantasy lineups confirmed are automatically entered into the next game week contest. Build your squad options by opening packs and acquiring cards in the marketplace using DD Coins.

HOW DO I GET MORE PACKS?

There are plenty of opportunities to get your hands on more packs.

• Head over to the marketplace and use your DD Coins to pick up rarity or positional packs.

• Login every day to accumulate rewards through the login reward benefit.

• Refer the game to friends and be rewarded with coins to drive your #DDSquad to glory!

HOW DO I GET MORE DD COINS?

DD Coins are the must have item to do your wheeling and dealing in the marketplace. Find your coin balance in your profile.

DD Coins can be acquired in-app, through the marketplace or via in-game features, or head over to our social channels to win coins in competitions and giveaways.

If you do purchase additional DD Coins, we strongly encourage you to control your spending and spend no more than you can afford. As per the Terms and Conditions, DD Coins (as well as packs and cards) have no monetary value and are not withdrawable or exchangeable for cash outside of DD. Coins should only be purchased for recreational use within DD and not for the purpose of making any profit or return.

WHAT DETERMINES IF A CARD IS GOLD, SILVER OR BASE?

The card level is determined by the DD Team before the season begins and this is not then altered as the season progresses. New incomings to the league are allocated a level as we see fit.

Gold, Silver and Base cards are more evenly split, with each making up approximately a third. The selection of each card is based on their previous season score, alongside expectation of their score for the season ahead.

WHAT HAPPENS IF I SIGN-UP MIDWAY THROUGH THE DURATION OF THE FANTASY GAME?

No problem! Your chances of winning start afresh every gameweek of the different competitions. Each gameweek is a separate contest where all users begin with zero points.

DO I OWN THE CARDS I'VE COLLECTED?

Cards collected are simply part of the game and are not owned outside of the game by users. You can use cards collected in your fantasy team or sell them on the marketplace for DD Coins; however, you cannot exchange or withdraw cards (or even DD Coins) for real money. At the end of the season, the game is reset and all cards are removed.

WHY DO I HAVE TO VERIFY MY EMAIL AND MOBILE NUMBER?

Email and mobile number verification is required for you to play Dhan Dhoom. This prevents a person creating multiple accounts, which for a number of reasons is strictly prohibited in the Terms and Conditions.
