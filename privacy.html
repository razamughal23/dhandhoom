5. YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS

In accordance with the GDPR and relevant Indian data protection laws, You are afforded specific rights concerning the processing of Your personal data, including the following:

- The right to access Your personal data, commonly known as a “data subject access request”. This right allows You to obtain a copy of the personal data We hold about You and to verify that We are processing it lawfully.

- The right to request that We correct or update any personal data We hold about You that is inaccurate or incomplete.

- The right to have Your personal data transferred to You or another party in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format, also known as “data portability”.

- The right to request the deletion of Your personal data when there is no valid reason for Us to continue processing it. You also have the right to request the deletion of Your personal data when You have objected to its processing.

- The right to request a halt on the processing of Your personal data, for instance, if You wish for Us to verify its accuracy or the reasons for processing it.

- The right to object to the processing of Your personal data under certain conditions, particularly if the processing is based on the legitimate interests pursued by Dhan Dhoom.

- The right to lodge a complaint with the relevant supervisory authority if You are dissatisfied with how We handle Your data. For residents in India, complaints can be directed to the appropriate local data protection authority.

These rights are not absolute and are subject to various conditions under the applicable data protection and privacy laws and regulations that govern us. If You decide You no longer want to be contacted for marketing purposes or wish to exercise any of Your rights as outlined above, please reach out to Us at support@dhandhoom.com. Accessing Your personal data (or exercising any other rights) will not incur a fee. However, should Your request for access be clearly unfounded or excessive, We may charge a reasonable fee or refuse to comply with the request under such circumstances.

Additionally, You retain the right to file a complaint with the Information Commissioner’s Office at any time if You are concerned about Our handling of Your personal data.

8. USE OF COOKIES

To enhance the functionality and user-friendliness of the Platform for Our Users, We employ "cookies" and similar digital mechanisms to gather data, assigning each visitor a distinct, anonymous identifier as a User Identification (User ID) to ascertain the User's specific interests via the identified device. Unless a User chooses to reveal their identity (for instance, by registering), Dhan Dhoom has no means to recognize the User, even if We allocate a cookie to the User's device. A cookie can only hold data provided by the User and is incapable of extracting information from the User's device hard drive. Advertisers on Dhan Dhoom may also set their own cookies on the User's device when the User engages with their advertisements, a practice over which Dhan Dhoom has no oversight.

Dhan Dhoom's servers automatically log limited details about the User's device's internet connection, which includes the User's IP address, during the User's interaction with the Platform. (An IP address is a numeric code that enables internet-connected devices to direct data, such as web pages the User requests, to the User). The User's IP address does not personally identify the User. Dhan Dhoom utilizes this information to serve web pages to Users upon request, customize its Platform based on User interests, assess traffic within the Platform, and inform advertisers of the geographic locations of Dhan Dhoom's visitors.
